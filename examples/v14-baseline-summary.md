# V14 Baseline Summary

This repository does not ship private render evidence or local machine paths. The reusable V14 lessons are encoded in `SKILL.md`, `references/`, and the audit scripts.

## Reusable Baseline

- DaVinci Resolve is the default finishing path.
- Opening and chapter titles must be clean, scenic, and free of duplicate route/date text.
- Scenic, title, and transition windows use BGM-only audio unless a deliberate exception is approved.
- Narration is exported as TXT/SRT when voiceover audio is rejected.
- Subtitle overlays must be dense enough for story guidance and suppressed around title-safe zones.
- Actual Resolve blueprint source paths are scanned for portrait/square/unknown orientation regressions.
- Opening, chapter, transition, and ending moments need local scenic footage or approved stock/aerial fallback evidence.
- Bilibili/reference-style checks require route texture, transport, street detail, lived-in beats, landmarks, pacing variety, and ending aftertaste.
- Final delivery must run the full QA suite, the skill maturity contract, and the V14 baseline contract.

## Public Evidence Policy

Generated QA reports often contain private source-media paths, external-drive names, project titles, and render locations. Keep those reports inside the user's delivery package and do not commit them to the public skill repository.
